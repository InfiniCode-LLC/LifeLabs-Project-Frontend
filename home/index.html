About this fundraiser

The Ripple Effect is a fundraiser founded by the InfiniLabs Group to help people in need in Sitio Bebe, with the lack of healthcare, and the imposed health risks. Our fundraiser features a concert where people can buy tickets that would then be donated to Sitio Bebe.
